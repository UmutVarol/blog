Welcome to My About Page

Hello, I'm Muhammed Umut Varol, a 21-year-old student currently studying Software Engineering at Okan University. As you may have seen from the posts on my blog, I am passionate about cryptocurrencies, cars, and football. I am originally from Iğdır, but I live in Istanbul, a city whose history and cultural significance I deeply admire.

I have two siblings and I'm the eldest. I care deeply for both of my younger siblings. One of my biggest aspirations is to bring about meaningful changes in areas that benefit humanity. Pursuing Software Engineering is, I believe, a great way to start achieving that dream. Another goal of mine is to travel the world—starting with visiting historical places and landmarks.

Describing myself, I am a dreamer, an enthusiastic, and an adventure-loving person. I thrive when I'm on the move; being inactive makes me feel quite the opposite—melancholic and restless. I live both my feelings and emotions to the fullest extent.

These are just a few things I can say about myself.
